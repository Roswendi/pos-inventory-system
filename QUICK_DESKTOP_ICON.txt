IF ICON DOESN'T APPEAR:
=======================

1. Refresh Desktop:
   - Press Cmd+Option+Esc
   - Select Finder
   - Click "Relaunch"

2. Or restart Finder:
   - Press Cmd+Option+Esc
   - Select Finder
   - Click "Force Quit"
   - Finder will restart automatically

3. Check Desktop folder:
   - Open Finder
   - Go to Desktop
   - Look for "TDO POS System.app"
